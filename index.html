Beat Master

Beat Master is a beat and rhythm app built for music lovers. It brings together a metronome, rhythm training, beat lessons, practice logs, and visual rhythm visualizations. Whether you’re a music beginner, drummer, guitarist, DJ, producer, or simply want to sharpen your sense of rhythm, you’ll find features and practice modes tailored for you.
