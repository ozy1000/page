Privacy Policy

Audio Layrs

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Introduction

Audio Layrs ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Audio Layrs handles information when you use the app on your iPhone or iPad.

Audio Layrs is a fully on-device app. All audio processing — including AI-powered stem separation — happens locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that receive your audio files or personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Audio Layrs does not:

  • Collect, transmit, or upload your audio files to any server
  • Require account creation, sign-in, or registration
  • Track your usage, behavior, or analytics
  • Use cookies, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies
  • Access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone
  • Share data with third parties

How Audio Layrs Works

Audio Layrs uses on-device machine learning models (Core ML) to separate songs into vocals, drums, bass, and instruments. All processing is performed locally using your device's Neural Engine, GPU, and CPU.

Your audio files never leave your device. No internet connection is required to use the core features of Audio Layrs.

Music Library Access

If you choose to import a song from your iOS Music Library, Audio Layrs will request the Media & Apple Music permission. This permission is used solely to let you select a song to import.

  • The selection happens through Apple's standard system picker
  • Audio Layrs only accesses the specific song you choose
  • DRM-protected tracks (such as Apple Music subscription content) cannot be imported and are filtered out automatically
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Media & Apple Music

Files & Local Storage

When you import an audio file (from Files, Music library, or URL), Audio Layrs stores a copy in the app's sandboxed storage directory on your device. This data is:

  • Stored exclusively on your local device
  • Accessible only to Audio Layrs (iOS App Sandbox)
  • Never transmitted over the network
  • Fully removable through the app's "Clear cache" function, by deleting individual tracks within the app, or by uninstalling Audio Layrs

Data Security

Because Audio Layrs runs entirely on your device and does not communicate with any external service for audio processing, your audio content and any derived data (separated stems, exported mixes) remain under your full control at all times.

Audio Layrs operates within the iOS App Sandbox, which restricts the app's access to system resources and ensures that other apps cannot access Audio Layrs's data.

Network Usage

Audio Layrs may use the internet only for the following optional, user-initiated actions:

  • Importing audio from a URL you paste into the app — the file is downloaded directly to your device and is not relayed through our servers
  • Sharing exported files using the iOS share sheet (e.g. AirDrop, Messages, third-party apps you choose)

Audio Layrs does not send your audio, usage data, or any personal information to any server controlled by us.

Children's Privacy

Audio Layrs does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Audio Layrs does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app uses Apple's first-party frameworks (Core ML, AVFoundation, MediaPlayer, SwiftUI), the open-source GRDB library for local database storage, and the open-source mobile-ffmpeg library for audio format conversion. None of these components transmit your data externally.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
