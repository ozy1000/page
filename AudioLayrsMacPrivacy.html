Privacy Policy

Audio Layrs

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Introduction

Audio Layrs ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Audio Layrs handles information when you use the app on your Mac.

Audio Layrs is a fully on-device app. All audio processing — including AI-powered stem separation — happens locally on your Mac. We do not operate any servers that receive your audio files or personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Audio Layrs does not:

  • Collect, transmit, or upload your audio files to any server
  • Require account creation, sign-in, or registration
  • Track your usage, behavior, or analytics
  • Use cookies, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies
  • Access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone
  • Share data with third parties

How Audio Layrs Works

Audio Layrs uses on-device machine learning models (Core ML) to separate songs into drums, bass, vocals, and accompaniment. All processing is performed locally using your Mac's Apple Silicon Neural Engine and GPU.

Your audio files never leave your Mac. No internet connection is required to use the core features of Audio Layrs.

Files & Local Storage

When you import an audio or video file, Audio Layrs stores a copy in the app's sandboxed storage directory on your Mac. This data is:

  • Stored exclusively on your local device
  • Accessible only to Audio Layrs (macOS App Sandbox)
  • Never transmitted over the network
  • Fully removable through the app's "Clear all data" function or by uninstalling Audio Layrs

Data Security

Because Audio Layrs runs entirely on your Mac and does not communicate with any external service, your audio content and any derived data (separated tracks, mixes) remain under your full control at all times.

Audio Layrs operates within the macOS App Sandbox, which restricts the app's access to system resources and ensures that other apps cannot access Audio Layrs's data.

Children's Privacy

Audio Layrs does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Audio Layrs does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app uses only Apple's first-party frameworks (Core ML, AVFoundation, SwiftUI) and the open-source GRDB library for local database storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
