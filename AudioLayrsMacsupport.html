Support

Audio Layrs splits any song into drums, bass, vocals, and accompaniment using on-device AI — fully private, no uploads required. Below are answers to common questions. If you don't find what you need, get in touch.

System Requirements

  • macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later
  • Apple Silicon Mac (M1 / M2 / M3 / M4 or later) — Intel Macs are not supported
  • At least 2 GB of free disk space for the AI models

Supported File Formats

  • Audio: mp3, wav, m4a, aac, aiff, flac
  • Video: mp4, mov, m4v (audio track is extracted automatically)
  • Export: M4A (AAC)

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Audio Layrs upload my songs to a server?

No. All processing happens locally on your Mac using Core ML and Apple Silicon. Your audio files never leave your device. The app also works fully offline.

Why is extraction grayed out on other tracks while one is running?

Audio Layrs extracts one stem at a time to keep memory usage stable. Once the current extraction finishes, the other Extract buttons become clickable again.

How long does extraction take?

On Apple Silicon, expect roughly 5–15 seconds for a 3-minute song. Faster Macs (M2, M3, M4) run noticeably quicker than M1.

Where are my extracted tracks stored?

Inside the app's sandboxed Application Support folder. Use the row's "···" menu → "Show original file in Finder" to locate the source. You can export any stem or your custom mix to a chosen location via the export button.

Can I export multiple stems as a single mix?

Yes. Set the volume / mute / solo on each track the way you want, then use Export Mix (M4A). Audio Layrs renders your custom mix to an M4A file with your exact settings applied.

Does Audio Layrs collect any data about me?

No analytics, no tracking, no account required. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Will you add support for Intel Macs?

No. The AI models require Apple Silicon Neural Engine / GPU to run at usable speed. Audio Layrs is built exclusively for M-series Macs.
