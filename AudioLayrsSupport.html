Support

Audio Layrs splits any song into vocals, drums, bass, and instruments using on-device AI — fully private, no uploads required. Below are answers to common questions. If you don't find what you need, get in touch.

System Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • iPhone with A14 Bionic or later (iPhone 12 and newer recommended for best performance)
  • At least 3.5 GB of available RAM for on-device AI processing
  • About 500 MB of free storage for the AI models and your imported tracks

Supported File Formats

  • Import: mp3, m4a, wav, aac, aiff, flac (from Files app, Music library, or URL)
  • Export: MP3 at 128 / 256 / 320 kbps

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Audio Layrs upload my songs to a server?

No. All processing happens locally on your iPhone using Core ML and the Neural Engine. Your audio files never leave your device. The core features work fully offline.

Why can't I import songs from Apple Music?

Apple Music subscription tracks are DRM-protected and cannot be exported by any app, including Audio Layrs. Only non-protected music in your library (such as files you've synced from your own collection) can be imported. The picker filters out protected tracks automatically.

Why is separation queued on other tracks while one is running?

Audio Layrs separates one song at a time to keep memory usage stable on iOS. Once the current separation finishes, the next queued track starts automatically.

How long does separation take?

Roughly 30–90 seconds for a 3-minute song, depending on your device. Newer iPhones with more powerful Neural Engines run noticeably faster.

What are M and S on each track?

M = Mute (silence that track). S = Solo (only the soloed tracks play, others are silenced). Solo supports multiple tracks at once — for example, Solo both Vocals and Drums to hear just those two.

How do I export my custom mix?

Adjust the volume, M, and S on each track until it sounds right. Tap the share button on a track row (or the export button in the full-screen player) to render your current mix to MP3 with your exact settings applied. The bitrate is set in Settings → Export Quality.

Does Audio Layrs collect any data about me?

No analytics, no tracking, no account required. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I clear cache or free up storage?

Go to Settings inside the app and tap "Clear cache". This removes temporary files and previously exported mixes, but keeps your imported songs and separated stems intact.

Does Audio Layrs work on iPad?

Yes. Audio Layrs runs on iPad with iPadOS 16 or later. Performance scales with the device — iPad Pro and iPad Air with M-series chips are especially fast.
